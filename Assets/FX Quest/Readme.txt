FX Quest v.0.5.5
------------------------------------------------------------------

	This package contains 187+ JRPG Particle Effects, ready to use with your 2D and 3D scenes.

	Features:

		ï 187+ Particle Effects for 2D and 3D scenes.
		ï 22 effects that can be used with UI.
		ï No pre-render, you can change their colors.
		ï All particles can be rescaled.
		ï Look nice even in dark or very bright scene.
		ï Sample Sound FX.
		ï 2D and 3D demo scenese with sample C# scripts.
		ï FX Quest Particle Scaler tool included.

		ï Support all build player platforms.
		
	Compatible:

		ï Unity 5.5.1 or higher.

	Note:

		ï Particle scaler tool can be used only in Unity Editor (limitation of Shuriken Particle architecture).
		ï To resize any particle to larger than 10 times of original size, you just have to rescale it to 10x at first time, save to a prefab. Select prefab and drop it into scene then rescale it again.
